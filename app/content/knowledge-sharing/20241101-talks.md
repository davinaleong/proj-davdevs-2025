## Featured Talks

### 1. Craft CMS - Singapore PHP User Group

**Event:** Singapore PHP User Group  
**Topic:** Introduction to Craft CMS and its ecosystem  
**Video:** [Watch on Engineers.sg](https://engineers.sg/video/craft-cms-singapore-php-user-group--3235)

In this presentation, I explored Craft CMS, a powerful content management system built on PHP. I covered:
- **What makes Craft CMS unique** in the crowded CMS landscape
- **Key features and capabilities** that developers and content creators love
- **Real-world use cases** and when to choose Craft over other solutions
- **Getting started** with development and deployment
- **Best practices** for building maintainable Craft websites

This talk was particularly meaningful as it combined my passion for PHP development with practical insights from client projects where Craft CMS proved to be the perfect solution.

### 2. Journey to a Healthy Lifestyle - JuniorDevSG

**Event:** JuniorDevSG  
**Topic:** Balancing health and wellness in a developer's life  
**Video:** [Watch on Engineers.sg](https://engineers.sg/video/journey-to-a-healthy-lifestyle-juniordevsg--3146)

This presentation took a more personal approach, sharing my journey towards maintaining a healthy lifestyle while working in tech. Key topics included:

- **The developer wellness challenge**: Common health issues in our industry
- **My personal transformation story**: From sedentary to active lifestyle
- **Practical strategies** for incorporating health habits into a busy dev schedule
- **Mental health awareness** and its importance in our profession
- **Work-life balance tips** that actually work for developers
- **Community and accountability** - how the dev community can support wellness

This talk resonated deeply with the JuniorDevSG community, sparking conversations about sustainable career practices and the importance of self-care in tech.
