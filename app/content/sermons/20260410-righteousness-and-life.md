> Proverbs 10:11 NKJV
> 
> 11 The mouth of the righteous is a well of life,<br>
> But violence covers the mouth of the wicked

Proverbs tells us that “the mouth of the righteous is a well of life”

Do you know who is the “righteous” mentioned here in this verse? It’s our Lord Jesus. He is righteous. And out of His mouth is a “well of life”

That should give you an indication of how to discern godly teachings. If they’re truly Jesus-cantered and Bible-based, they will be a “well of life” and you will find life in their teachings

So, it’s important to sit under ministries that are wells of life, amen!
