> Proverbs 3:1-2 NKJV
> 
> 1 My son, do not forget my law,<br>
> But let your heart keep my commands;<br>
> 2 For length of days and long life<br>
> And peace they will add to you.

Your Abba constantly reminds you in all your getting, get wisdom. Why? Because wisdom comes with its promises and benefits. 

As stated in these two verses, when you do not forget God’s law, or in this age, the word that’s fed to us every Sunday, and keep the commandments that the Holy Spirit writes on your heart, they will add length of days and long life to you. Peace is also added to you too!

So, keep seeking the Lord for wisdom, amen!
