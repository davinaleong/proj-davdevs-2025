> Proverbs 10:30 NKJV
> 
> 30 The righteous will never be removed,<br>
> But the wicked will not inhabit the earth.

Here in the book of wisdom, which is the book of Proverbs, it says that the “righteous will never be removed”

Do you know who the " righteous " is mentioned here in this verse? It’s Jesus. And by faith in Him, YOU!

And do you know what God’s promise to the righteous? They will “never be removed”. That’s why Jesus’ throne is established forever. And to those who believe in Him, believers like you, will never be removed, either because you are in Him and He has given you His righteousness. 

So, be reassured that your salvation is in heaven and can never be removed, amen!
