> Proverbs 10:27 NKJV
> 
> 27 The fear of the Lord prolongs days,<br>
> But the years of the wicked will be shortened.

Do you want to live long? The secret is to fear (or revere) the Lord

When you fear the Lord, you’ll start to treasure His presence. You’ll start to value His word and desire to follow what He writes on your heart.

When you flow with Jesus and stop loving the world, it comes with a promise. And God’s promises are sure and guaranteed. I mean, what are man’s promises compared to God’s right?

So, follow Jesus and your days shall be prolonged, amen!
