> Psalm 34:15 NKJV
>
> And His ears are open to their cry.

When you accept Jesus as Your Lord and Saviour, His blood washes you clean of all your sins. On top of that, He gives you the gift of no condemnation and righteousness. 

Which means, as a believer now, you are righteous by faith in Jesus. It’s His obedience that God puts to your account and sees you perfectly righteous. 
How do all these revelations tie into today’s verse? The Lord’s eyes are on you. His ears are open to your cry. 

So, don’t worry little sheep! Cast all your cares to Jesus, Your Lord and Shepherd. Even a groan will reach the throne. You don’t have to say elaborate prayers. Just simple prayers like, “Jesus, help me!” Or “Abba, I’m afraid!”. Tell Him. He’s your Lord, your Shepherd and your Daddy God!

Let me reiterate again. His eyes are on YOU, you righteous one in Christ! YOUR GOD’s ears are open to your cry. So, keep crying out to Him in your times of need, trouble or trial, amen!
