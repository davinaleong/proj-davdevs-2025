> Proverbs 9:11 NKJV
> 
> 11 For by me your days will be multiplied,<br>
> And years of life will be added to you.

Do you want to live long? Well the secret is found in Proverbs 9. 

In verse 11, it says that “For by me (wisdom) your days will be multiplied”. So, living by God’s wisdom will cause your days to be multiplied. Then, just in case you don’t get it, Daddy God repeats it again to bring home the point. Walk in wisdom, and “years of life will be added to you”. Notice, it’s not “days”, but “years of life” “added” to you. 

Wow! Not only does godly wisdom cause your days to be multiplied, but also adds years to your life!

So, the secret to having long life is walking in godly wisdom, amen!
