## The Three Formatting Champions üèÜ

### 1. Bold Text: The Attention-Grabber ‚≠ê

**The secret character:** Asterisk (`*`)

**How to use it:** Wrap your text in asterisks
- Type: `I love *pizza*`
- Sends as: I love **pizza**

![Screenshot of Bold WhatsApp Text](/articles/20240917-0006-how-to-bold-italicize-and-strikethrough-text-in-whatsapp.PNG)

**Perfect for:** Important announcements, emphasis, making key words pop!

### 2. Italic Text: The Subtle Stylist ‚ú®

**The secret character:** Underscore (`_`)

**How to use it:** Wrap your text in underscores
- Type: `I am _so excited_`
- Sends as: I am *so excited*

![Screenshot of Italicized WhatsApp Text](/articles/20240917-0007-how-to-bold-italicize-and-strikethrough-text-in-whatsapp.png)

**Perfect for:** Adding emotion, subtle emphasis, or a touch of elegance!

### 3. Strikethrough Text: The Playful Editor ‚ùå

**The secret character:** Tilde (`~`) - The squiggly line!

**How to use it:** Wrap your text in tildes
- Type: `I used to be ~29 years old~`
- Sends as: I used to be ~~29 years old~~

![Screenshot of Strikethrough WhatsApp Text](/articles/20240917-0008-how-to-bold-italicize-and-strikethrough-text-in-whatsapp.png)

**Perfect for:** Corrections, playful thoughts, showing "before and after" ideas!

## Pro Tips for Formatting Like a Boss üéØ

### Mix and Match Magic
You can combine these formatting styles:
- `*Bold* and _italic_ and ~strikethrough~`
- `_*Bold italic*_ text` (order matters!)

### When to Use Each Style

**Bold (`*text*`):**
- Important deadlines or dates
- Key information in group chats
- Emphasis in professional messages

**Italic (`_text_`):**
- Adding emotion or mood
- Foreign words or technical terms
- Subtle emphasis without shouting

**Strikethrough (`~text~`):**
- Playful corrections
- Showing old vs. new information
- Adding humor to your messages

## Quick Troubleshooting üîß

**Characters showing up instead of formatting?**
- Make sure there are **no spaces** between the special characters and your text
- Check that you're using the correct characters (asterisk, underscore, tilde)

**Not working on certain words?**
- Some words with apostrophes or special characters might need extra attention
- Try formatting shorter phrases first to test
