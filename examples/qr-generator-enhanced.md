## QR Code Generator

Create custom QR codes instantly with our easy-to-use generator tool. Perfect for sharing links, text, contact information, and more.

<!-- Component will be rendered above -->

### Features

- **Quick Generation**: Instant QR code creation
- **Customizable Size**: Choose from multiple size options
- **Error Correction**: Multiple error correction levels
- **Download Options**: Save as PNG or SVG
- **Logo Support**: Add custom logos to your QR codes

### How to Use

1. **Enter Content**: Type or paste the text, URL, or information you want to encode
2. **Customize Settings**: 
   - Select your preferred size (128px to 512px)
   - Choose error correction level (L, M, Q, H)
   - Optionally add a logo
3. **Generate**: Click the generate button to create your QR code
4. **Download**: Save your QR code in your preferred format

### Use Cases

**Business Applications:**
- Contact information sharing
- Product links and catalogs
- Event registration
- WiFi network sharing

**Personal Use:**
- Social media profiles
- Personal websites
- Gift messages
- Location sharing

### Technical Details

Our QR code generator supports:
- **Text Encoding**: UTF-8 text up to 2,953 characters
- **URL Encoding**: Web links and contact information
- **Error Correction**: Redundancy levels from 7% to 30%
- **Output Formats**: PNG (raster) and SVG (vector)

### Tips for Better QR Codes

1. **Keep It Simple**: Shorter text creates cleaner, more scannable codes
2. **Test Thoroughly**: Always test your QR codes with multiple devices
3. **Consider Context**: Ensure sufficient contrast for scanning
4. **Size Matters**: Larger codes are easier to scan from distance
