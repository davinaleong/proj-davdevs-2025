> Proverbs 10:22 NKJV
> 
> 22 The blessing of the Lord makes one rich,<br/>
> And He adds no sorrow with it.

Do you know there’s a difference between worldly and godly blessings? Worldly blessings may make one rich but bring sorrow with them.

But blessings from the Lord make one rich without adding sorrow with it. 

So, let’s keep asking, seeking, and knocking for godly blessings from the Lord, amen!
